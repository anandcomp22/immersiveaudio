Nikhil Yadav

Audio Engineer / Designer

Hi, I’m Nikhil Yadav, an Audio Engineer and Designer passionate about bringing stories to life through sound. Based in Pune, India, I’ve worked on projects ranging from feature films and audiobooks to web series and video games. I started my journey with a Diploma in Sound Engineering and have since collaborated with creative teams to craft immersive audio experiences. As a Senior Audio Engineer at Pocket FM, Germany, I lead a talented team, designing soundscapes, Foley effects, and mixing audio that connects emotionally with audiences. I love experimenting with cutting-edge tools like Pro Tools, Unreal Engine, and Dolby Atmos to create memorable soundtracks. Whether it’s capturing the perfect ambience, enhancing storytelling, or delivering clean, professional audio, I’m always up for a creative challenge. Let’s connect and do something extraordinary together!
